FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Still unsure? Book a call and we'll answer everything for your specific market.

Will this work for my B2B business? +

We don't take on clients unless we're reasonably confident we can deliver results. Since our pricing is performance-based, that confidence is built into the engagement from day one.

How many leads or booked meetings can I expect per month? +

This depends on your sales goals and the nature of your target market. We structure campaigns around the number of leads or meetings you can realistically handle each week or month.

What's the price per lead or booked meeting, and when am I charged? +

Pricing depends on your target market and how easily appointments can be set within it. We aim to structure pricing so it works in your favour — get on a call with us and we'll work through it together.

If I opt for booked meetings, am I charged for no-shows? +

No. You are not charged for prospects who don't show up to a booked meeting.

Can I slow down or speed up the flow of leads at any time? +

Once campaigns are live they run continuously, but we'll always do our best to accommodate a change in pace when you give us reasonable notice.

Are there any time-bound contracts? +

No fixed-term contract is required. There is a minimum number of appointments agreed in the initial scope before an engagement begins.

Are there alternate pricing options? +

Yes. We also offer monthly retainer pricing, with a set number of prospects contacted each month and a reasonable expectation of results agreed up front.
